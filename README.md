# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

T&E Monsters Learn Together ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our application.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Our app does not require users to create accounts and does not request names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal information.

## Microphone Usage

The application may use the device microphone for speech recognition and educational purposes.

Voice input is used only to help children practice speech and language skills. Audio recordings are not stored by us and are not shared with third parties.

## Advertising

Our application does not contain advertisements.

## Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share user information with third parties.

Some speech recognition functionality may rely on services provided by the device operating system or Google services.

## Children's Privacy

This application is designed for children and families.

We are committed to complying with applicable laws and regulations regarding children's privacy, including COPPA and GDPR where applicable.

## Data Security

We do not collect personal information. Therefore, no personal data is stored on our servers.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
